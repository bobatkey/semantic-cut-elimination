  _&_  : Formula → Formula → Formula

Negation:
\begin{displaymath}
  \begin{array}{lcl}
    (a)^\perp           & = & a^\perp                     \\
    (a^\perp)^\perp     & = & a                           \\
    (I)^\perp           & = & I                           \\
    (P \otimes Q)^\perp & = & (P)^\perp \parr   (Q)^\perp \\
    (P \parr   Q)^\perp & = & (P)^\perp \otimes (Q)^\perp \\
    (P \with   Q)^\perp & = & (P)^\perp \oplus  (Q)^\perp \\
    (P \oplus  Q)^\perp & = & (P)^\perp \with   (Q)^\perp \\
    (P ; Q)^\perp       & = & (P)^\perp ; (Q)^\perp
  \end{array}
\end{displaymath}
\begin{code}
¬ : Formula → Formula
¬ I        = I
¬ (+ a)    = - a
¬ (- a)    = + a
¬ (p ⅋ q)  = ¬ p ⊗ ¬ q
¬ (p ⊗ q)  = ¬ p ⅋ ¬ q
¬ (p & q)  = ¬ p ⊕ ¬ q
¬ (p ⊕ q)  = ¬ p & ¬ q
¬ (p ▷ q)  = ¬ p ▷ ¬ q
\end{code}
